Configure BAM

(Category: Business Activity Monitoring)

Business Activity Monitoring (BAM) allows you to track key performance indicators (KPIs) of business processes.

Design the type of reports you want to display for KPI data using Jaspersoft or Eclipse Business Intelligence and Reporting Tools (BIRT). These reports can then be installed into Bonita User Experience and shown in the Bonita User Experience dashboard.

Create a KPI report from inside Bonita Studio, or import or export a report.
